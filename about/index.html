Who We Are

About The Free Security

The Free Security is a non-profit initiative dedicated to improving cybersecurity for businesses that lack the budget for security assessments. At the same time, we provide aspiring penetration testers with real-world experience, helping them develop their skills and enter the industry.

Our Mission

We believe that security should be accessible to all businesses, regardless of their financial capabilities. By connecting businesses in need with security enthusiasts looking for hands-on experience, we create a win-win solution that enhances overall cybersecurity.

How We Work

  • For Businesses: If your company lacks a cybersecurity budget, we offer free penetration testing to identify vulnerabilities and help you secure your systems.
  • For Aspiring Pentesters: Gain real-world experience by working on actual security assessments, guided by experienced professionals.

Why Choose Us?

  • Free cybersecurity assessments for businesses in need
  • Hands-on training for future penetration testers
  • A growing network of security professionals and ethical hackers
